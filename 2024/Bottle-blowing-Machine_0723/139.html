instructions

 Product Description:
Jiangsu Xinzi Whale Machinery Manufacturing Group (KIWL), with its deep accumulation and continuous innovation in the field of packaging machinery, grand launch of the "L-shaped film packer". Designed to improve packaging efficiency and flexibility, the unique L-shaped working path enables a smooth transition and efficient packaging of products in the packaging process, making it an indispensable packaging tool in modern production lines.
Key features:
Efficient packaging process: The L-shaped layout optimizes the packaging path, so that the product can maintain efficient operation in all aspects of transportation, positioning, film packaging, etc., significantly improving the overall packaging speed and production efficiency.
Flexible application: The film packer supports a variety of specifications, shapes of product packaging, through simple adjustment can be adapted to the packaging needs of different products to meet the diversified needs of the market.
Excellent packaging quality: High precision control system is used to ensure that the film material is evenly stretched and tightly fitted to the product during the packaging process, forming a beautiful and stable L-shaped packaging effect, effectively protecting the product from external pollution and damage.
Intelligent operation: Integrated advanced PLC control system and touch screen operation interface, to achieve rapid setting and adjustment of packaging parameters, while providing real-time monitoring and fault diagnosis functions, reduce the difficulty of operation, improve the intelligent level of production management.
Energy saving and environmental protection design: Machine design focuses on energy saving and consumption reduction, using high-efficiency motor and optimized transmission system to reduce energy consumption; At the same time, support the use of environmentally friendly packaging materials to help enterprises achieve green production.
Application field:
Widely used in food, beverage, daily chemical, medicine, hardware and other industries of L-shaped packaging needs, to provide products with efficient, beautiful, stable packaging solutions, enhance product market competitiveness.
Conclusion:
Jiangsu Xinzi Whale Machinery Manufacturing Group (KIWL) L-type film machine, with its efficient, flexible, intelligent, environmentally friendly excellent performance, has become the first choice for many enterprises to upgrade packaging. We will continue to adhere to the "innovation, quality, service" business philosophy, to provide customers with more quality packaging equipment and services, create a better future.
